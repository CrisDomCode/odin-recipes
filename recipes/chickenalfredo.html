Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken Alfredo is a comforting and indulgent pasta dish made with tender chicken breast, creamy Alfredo sauce, and fettuccine noodles. This classic Italian-American dish is perfect for a weeknight dinner or special occasion.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 Â½ cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all your ingredients.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook fettuccine in boiling water until al dente, about 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chicken strips and cook until golden brown and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Pour in heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens slightly.
  6. Stir in Parmesan cheese and continue to cook until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
  7. Add the cooked fettuccine and chicken back to the skillet, tossing to coat the pasta and chicken with the Alfredo sauce.
  8. Serve immediately, garnishing with chopped parsley if desired.
